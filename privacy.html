Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 3, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MindGlow. Your privacy and trust are paramount to us. This Privacy Policy explains how MindGlow ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our iOS mobile application.

MindGlow is a mental wellness application designed to support your mental health journey through AI-powered conversations, mood tracking, task management, and evidence-based resources. We are deeply committed to protecting your sensitive personal and health information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and authentication credentials when you create an account through Firebase Authentication
  • Mood Data: Your mood ratings, emotional states, and related notes that you log in the app
  • Task Information: Tasks you create, including titles, descriptions, priorities, deadlines, and completion status
  • Assessment Responses: Your answers to mental health screening tools including PHQ-9 (depression), GAD-7 (anxiety), and PSS (stress) assessments
  • AI Chat Conversations: Messages you send to Milo, our AI companion, including text content and conversation history
  • Affirmation Preferences: Categories and types of affirmations you prefer or interact with
  • Journal Entries: Any notes, reflections, or journal content you create within the app

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: iOS version, device model, app version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, time spent in app, interaction patterns, and navigation paths
  • Crash Reports: Technical diagnostics and error logs to improve app stability
  • Subscription Data: Purchase history, subscription status, and payment information (processed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat)

üîí What We DON'T Collect

  • We do NOT collect your precise GPS location
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal files without explicit permission
  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do NOT use your health data for advertising purposes
  • We do NOT share identifiable information with data brokers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Services: Enable mood tracking, task management, AI conversations, and mental health assessments
  • Personalization: Customize affirmations, insights, and recommendations based on your usage patterns
  • AI Functionality: Process your chat messages with Milo using OpenAI's GPT models to provide supportive, empathetic responses
  • Analytics & Improvement: Analyze aggregated, anonymized usage data to improve app features and user experience
  • Account Management: Authenticate users, manage subscriptions, and provide customer support
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

4. Data Storage & Security

Security Measures

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text; we use Firebase's secure authentication
  • Most sensitive data (mood logs, tasks, assessments) is stored locally on your device using iOS's encrypted storage
  • We implement industry-standard security practices including regular security audits
  • Access to backend systems is restricted to essential personnel only

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 OpenAI (Milo AI Chat)

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

For All Users:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format

For EU/EEA Users (GDPR):

  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

For California Users (CCPA):

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@mindglow.app
